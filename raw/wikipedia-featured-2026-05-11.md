## 今日精選文章：Japan Cup

The Japan Cup  is a Group 1 horse race in Japan, held annually on the last Sunday of November, post time of 15:40 at Tokyo Racecourse in Fuchū, Tokyo. It is a flat race run over a distance of 2,400 metres with a maximum of 18 horses.
